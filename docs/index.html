About me

Guilherme Ferreira de Moraes, 21, São Paulo - Brazil.
Most of the things I build come from curiosity. I like understanding how systems work and then finding ways to make them simpler or more useful. That often leads me to problems involving data, automation, or connecting different tools and services. Lately I’ve been especially interested in geographic data, AI applied to real-world problems, and mathematical approaches to modeling or analysis. I also enjoy exploring systems in a hands-on, hacker-ish way. Outside of programming, music and traveling are two things that keep feeding that same curiosity.

Tools and references for usability, transparency, and accessibility in web design that I used when building this portfolio.
